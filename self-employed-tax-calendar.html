Helpful Tips

  • Make estimated payments on IRS Direct Pay.
  • Most self-employed taxpayers make quarterly estimated payments to avoid IRS penalties.
  • Use your 1099 income + deductible expenses to estimate your quarterly payments.
  • Don't forget state tax deadlines—check your state’s revenue department.
