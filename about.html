About DIY Money Tools

DIY Money Tools was created to help freelancers, contractors, and small business owners simplify their finances. Our goal is to provide easy-to-use calculators that help you save smarter, plan ahead, and make informed financial decisions — no spreadsheet required.

We’re continuously building new tools for budgeting, taxes, debt payoff, and savings so you can spend less time doing math and more time growing your business or enjoying life.

We believe financial tools should be simple, free, and accessible to everyone.
