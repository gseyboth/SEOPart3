How to Convert Hourly Pay to Salary (With Examples)

Wondering what your hourly pay translates to in yearly salary? It’s a common question for freelancers, gig workers, and anyone considering a new job offer.

The Basic Formula

Take your hourly wage, multiply by 40 hours/week, then by 52 weeks/year. For example:

$20/hour × 40 × 52 = $41,600/year

What to Consider

  • Are you paid for holidays and time off?
  • Do you work overtime?
  • Are you self-employed (and paying self-employment tax)?
