Helpful Tips

  • Make estimated payments using IRS Direct Pay.
  • Most self-employed workers make quarterly estimated payments to avoid IRS penalties.
  • Track your 1099 income and deductible expenses to estimate your quarterly payments.
  • Don't forget state tax deadlines—check your state’s revenue department website.
