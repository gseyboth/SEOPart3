Debt Snowball vs Avalanche: Which Should You Use?

Trying to pay off debt? Two of the most popular strategies are the Debt Snowball and the Debt Avalanche methods. But which one is right for you?

Snowball Method

With the snowball method, you pay off the smallest balance first to gain momentum. It’s great for motivation and quick wins.

Avalanche Method

The avalanche method pays off the highest-interest debt first. It saves more money in the long run but takes discipline.

Which Is Better?

If you're driven by psychology, choose Snowball. If you’re focused on math and savings, go Avalanche.
