Self-Employed Tax Calendar

Managing self-employment taxes can feel overwhelming, but our Self-Employed Tax Calendar breaks down key quarterly deadlines, making tax season easier to handle. If you're also figuring out how much to save, try our Savings Goal Calculator.

Quarterly Tax Deadlines for 2025

  • Q1: April 15, 2025 (for income from Jan–Mar)
  • Q2: June 17, 2025 (for income from Apr–May)
  • Q3: September 16, 2025 (for income from Jun–Aug)
  • Q4: January 15, 2026 (for income from Sep–Dec)

Who Needs to Pay Quarterly Taxes?

If you're self-employed, freelance, or run a small business and expect to owe $1,000 or more in taxes, you're likely required to pay estimated quarterly taxes. This includes federal income tax, self-employment tax (Social Security and Medicare), and possibly state tax depending on where you live.

Tips to Stay on Track

  • Set calendar reminders for each payment deadline.
  • Use accounting software or apps that automate tracking.
  • Consider saving ~25–30% of income for tax payments.
  • Use the Contractor Tax Estimator to calculate how much to pay each quarter.
